About Us

Ecoshare is the leading provider of bike share programs for cities, universities, businesses and properties.

We simplify bike sharing through a full-service model that covers everything involved in planning, building and operating a system. By using right-sized infrastructure and app-based technology, Ecoshare bike sharing programs are flexible and affordable for both the communities we serve and the riders within them

WE BELIEVE IN GREAT IDEAS

We believe future belong to smart, healthy and connected cities. India has takes a big step to build 100 smart cities and we are helping to build smart, affordable and eco-friendly urban transportation system.

50% of all auto trips are less then 3 miles, yet we all still hop in a car for a solo trip to our destination leading to mass congestion on our over-burdened transportation infrastructure.

Ecoshare bike sharing system enables municipalities, corporations, universities and many other enterprises to offer secure, emission free and economical first/last mile transport solutions.
